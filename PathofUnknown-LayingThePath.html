The Fall of Ogruk Bonezbreaker Archived Session
The Hook: Gregar enlisted the party to eliminate Ogruk Bonezbreaker after a wounded Dwarf Slayer (Ironhammer) arrived at the outpost, revealing Ogruk's goblins had wiped out the rest of his band.

The Approach: Setting out before sunrise, the party ambushed a roaming band of Beastmen. Tom acted as bait, blinding them with light magic before the rest of the party leapt from the rocks to finish the hooved beasts. Approaching the Worlds Edge Mountains, goblin lookouts struck Zell and Chris with javelins (Chris suffered a critical hand wound, but kept all his fingers!).

The Camp Assault: The party funneled into a narrow mountain passage. Ogruk attempted to goad the Slayer into single combat, but chaos erupted as two Ogre Henchmen and a horde of goblins charged.
  • The Fire & The Bear: The Goblin Shaman dropped a Firewall, catching the Flagellant, Warrior Priest, Bounty Hunter, and Pit Fighter ablaze. The Bounty Hunter and Flagellant had to stop, drop, and roll, while the Druid (Andy) shifted into a massive Bear and scaled the mountain walls to bypass the chokepoint.
  • The Meat Grinder: The Pit Fighter and Warrior Priest became locked in a war of attrition, at one point casually tanking five goblins at once with near-impenetrable iron armor.
  • The Climax: Ogruk, wielding a mammoth-bone hammer, was healed for 10 wounds by his Shaman. Enraged, Ogruk battered the Slayer into the dirt, and then struck down the Flagellant. With the frontline collapsed, the High Elf (Chris) desperately channeled healing magic. The Bear-Druid crashed through the flames, mauling the Ogre boss, but it was the Bounty Hunter (Erik Rose) who ended the fight, sinking a perfect arrow into Ogruk's chest!

The Aftermath: The Shaman fled into the mountains. The Elf revived the fallen, and the party returned to the outpost to claim their 50 Gold reward.

Loot, Upgrades & Altdorf Staging:
  • Erik Rose: Looted magical Knuckledusters (+20 Str, +2 Dmg, -2 Armor Pen) and a Magical Flail (Degeneration quality: 1 wound/turn until healed). Seeking Sigmar followers to employ in combat (Requires Fellowship roll).
  • Tom & Kyle: Reached Level 3 Wizards! Visiting the Altdorf academy to acquire new magic items and light warhorses (Requires upkeep and Riding/Animal Care skills).
  • Side Quest: Haggling with an Altdorf merchant for a +1 AP Ring. He will drop the price from 110g to 50g in exchange for 4 Large Wolf Pelts from the Grey Mountains.

Advanced Vocations

Advanced Vocations are the pinnacle of martial, magical, and tactical mastery. They represent a character moving beyond basic proficiency to become a true force on the battlefield.

The Path of Iron (Physique & Melee)

Battle-Ready XP: Compounding Base | Action: 1 Minor | Min. Req: Agility 6+ OR Awareness 6+

The veteran warrior does not see a chaotic melee, but a predictable rhythm of striking blades and shifting weight. By controlling the space around them, they turn a swarm of enemies into a series of manageable, isolated duels.

Mechanic: While this stance is active, you automatically negate the numerical advantage (flanking, swarming, or outnumbering bonuses) of up to three opponents engaging you in melee from your front or sides.

Vulnerability: The stance breaks down if you are completely surrounded. Attacks originating directly from behind, or any attacks made by a fourth simultaneous combatant, bypass this skill.

Specialized Stance: Berserker Actions: 1 | Prerequisites: Unlocked via Battle-Ready

Some warriors fight to live; others live only to kill. Casting aside all pretense of self-preservation, you channel your fury into a reckless, devastating offensive.

  • You ignore any disadvantage penalties to your melee to-hit rolls for the turn.
  • Any successful melee strike inflicts your normal weapon wounds plus an additional 1d10 wounds.
  • Brutal Strike (Crit): On an unmodified 10, the target takes normal wounds plus 2d10 additional wounds.

Vulnerability: You suffer a flat -1 penalty to your Defense while active.

Cleave XP: 100 | Actions: 1 | Min. Req: Heavy/2H Weapon, Physique (3)

Focus is usually a warrior’s greatest strength, but against a horde, it is a death sentence. You have learned to stop treating enemies like individuals and start treating them like a harvest.

Passive: +1 Wound Bonus when fighting 2 or more enemies.

Mechanic: The Sweeping Arc

  • The Strike: If you successfully hit an enemy, you may apply the same To-Hit roll to a second enemy adjacent to the first.
  • The Damage: Primary target takes Normal Wounds. Secondary target takes (1/2) Normal Wounds.
Specialization: Whirlwind Actions: 3 (Full Action Pool)

You become a centrifugal force of destruction. There is no front, no back, and no flank—only a circle of steel that belongs to you.

  • The Hurricane: You make a single To-Hit roll at Disadvantage applied to every enemy within reach.
  • The Damage: Wounds = Normal + (2 x Physique).
  • The Eye of the Storm (Crit): All targets are knocked Prone and suffer an additional 1d10 Wounds.
Cleric XP: 100 | Actions: 1 | Min. Req: Faith (3), Light Soul (2)

You are the beacon in the encroaching dark. Where others see only the terrifying power of the unholy, you see a shadow that must be banished.

  • Sanctified Essence: Gain a permanent +1 Wound Mitigation (WM) against all sources.
  • Vessel of Restoration: Recover +1 additional Wound when healed by a Blessing.

The Holy Chant: Activate a 60ft aura. Allies gain +1 to Fear tests. Against "Evil" targets, your WM is increased by your Light Soul points.

  • Divine Riposte (Crit): Against "Evil" natural 10s: WM = (Light Soul Points x 2).
Specialization: Crusader Actions: 1 (Crusader's Chant)

Divine justice is not found in a prayer book; it is carved into the hides of the wicked. You do not merely ward off the dark—you hunt it down.

  • Righteous Terror: Force a Fear test (DV 5) to all "Evil" enemies within your aura.
  • Hammer of the Wicked: Deal additional Wounds against "Evil" targets equal to your Light Soul points.
  • Divine Execution (Crit): On an unmodified 10: Bonus Wounds = (Light Soul Points x 2).
Command XP: 100 | Actions: 1 | Min. Req: Charisma (3), Leadership (2)

A battle is a machine, and you are the one pulling the levers. With a single shouted order, you can push an exhausted ally to strike one last time.

Passive: +1 to all Fear/Morale tests for allies within 30 feet.

  • The Directive: Spend 1 Action to grant an ally 1 Action Point (AP) to use immediately. They gain your Charisma Bonus to their next roll.
  • Perfect Order (Crit): The ally gains 2 AP instead.
Specialization: Blood Rally Actions: 2 (Focused Command)

Men do not die when their bodies fail; they die when they accept defeat.

  • The Rally: Target instantly removes 1 Stunned or Fear condition.
  • The Second Wind: If target is at 0 Wounds, they recover: Healing = 1d10 + (Charisma x 2).
  • Vanguard (Crit): Ally sheds all Stunned conditions and refreshes their entire 3-AP pool.

Precision is for scholars. You want power—raw, surging, and uncontrollable. You push your mind to the very brink of collapse.

  • Chain Reaction: Natural 10s trigger surges that can add infinite stacks of 1d10 wounds.

Specialization: Divergence Actions: 2 (Channeling)

The caster is adept at splitting their spell casting or blessings between multiple targets, friend or foe.

  • The Split Weave: Divide the Base Result among targets; then add your full static bonuses to each target individually.
  • The Calculation: Wounds (per target) = (Normal + 1d10) / Number of Targets.

Specialization: Sanctity Actions: 2 (Channeling) | Min. Req: Faith (9)
